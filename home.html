Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

How does your growth system approach differ from traditional marketing?

Unlike traditional marketing that focuses on disconnected tactics, our growth systems approach integrates acquisition, conversion, and retention strategies into a comprehensive revenue architecture. We use data-driven methodologies to identify your ideal customer profiles, build predictable acquisition channels, optimize conversion pathways, and maximize customer lifetime value – all working together as a unified system rather than siloed activities.

What types of businesses do you typically work with?

We specialize in working with B2B technology companies, SaaS platforms, and knowledge-based service businesses. Our clients typically have established products or services generating at least £500K in annual revenue and are looking to scale predictably. We've had particularly strong results with companies in fintech, edtech, martech, healthcare technology, and professional services sectors.

How long does it take to see results from your strategies?

Most clients begin seeing initial improvements within 30-45 days of implementation. Our strategic analysis and growth architecture typically takes 2-3 weeks to develop, followed by implementation and optimization. Significant, transformative results typically emerge around the 90-day mark as our systems begin working together and we optimize based on performance data. For enterprise clients with complex sales cycles, full realization of results may take 4-6 months.

What is your typical engagement process?

Our process begins with a comprehensive strategic analysis, where we examine your current business model, customer journey, acquisition channels, and conversion systems. We then develop a tailored growth architecture blueprint specific to your business objectives. After approval, we move to implementation, which includes setting up acquisition channels, optimizing conversion systems, and establishing performance metrics. Finally, we enter the continuous optimization phase, where we refine the system based on real-world data to maximize ROI.

How do you measure success and ROI?

We establish clear KPIs at the beginning of each engagement tied directly to revenue impact. Common metrics include customer acquisition cost (CAC), conversion rates at each stage of the funnel, customer lifetime value (LTV), and overall return on advertising spend (ROAS). We provide transparent reporting dashboards that track these metrics in real-time, allowing you to see exactly how our systems are performing and the return on your investment. Our benchmark is achieving at least a 3:1 return on investment across our engagements.
